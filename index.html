About This Story

This website is a digital storytelling project exploring the relationship between urban development and environmental sustainability in Kenya. Through images, narrative writing, and observation, the project highlights the contrast between fast growing cities and the natural environments that support life. The goal is to understand whether development and conservation can exist together.

This project demonstrates how digital media can be used to tell environmental stories through web design, photography, and structured narrative presentation.
